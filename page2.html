Housing Market Pressures

Australia's housing prices have reached historic highs, making home ownership or even renting unaffordable for many. This drives inequality and puts strain on vulnerable communities.

Combined with rising grocery, fuel, and utility costs, families are facing tough choices just to make ends meet.
