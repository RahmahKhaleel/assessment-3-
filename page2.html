Impact on Household Budgets

Exploring how the rising cost of living strains household finances and alters spending habits across Australia.

Changing Spending Patterns

Many families are cutting back on non-essential expenses, focusing primarily on necessities like food, housing, and utilities.

Debt levels have increased as households use credit to bridge gaps in income and expenses.

Government support programs aim to alleviate some of these pressures but are often seen as temporary fixes.
