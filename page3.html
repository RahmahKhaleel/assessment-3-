Government Responses and Policies

An overview of government measures aimed at addressing the cost of living crisis and supporting affected Australians.

Support Programs and Initiatives

The government has introduced various support packages, including energy bill subsidies, housing assistance, and income support.

Efforts to control inflation through monetary policy aim to stabilise prices, but impacts may take time to materialise.

Community organisations also play a vital role in providing immediate relief to vulnerable populations.
