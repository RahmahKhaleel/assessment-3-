The Rising Cost of Energy

Electricity and gas prices have increased significantly in recent years, making it harder for families to manage essential household expenses. This particularly affects low-income and vulnerable groups.

With global supply issues and increased demand, the pressure on Australia's energy market continues to affect affordability for consumers.
