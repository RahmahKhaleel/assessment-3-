Social Consequences of the Cost of Living Crisis

Understanding how economic challenges affect mental health, relationships, and overall community wellbeing.

Rising Stress and Anxiety

The financial pressures of increasing costs are leading to higher levels of stress and mental health struggles across the population.

Families face tough choices regarding basic needs, which can strain relationships and overall wellbeing.

Support services and community programs are vital to help individuals navigate these challenges, but more long-term solutions are needed.
